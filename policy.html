Policies

  • Ladies only (16+ years).
  • Any disrespectful or inappropriate behaviour will result in treatments being abandoned/cancelled.
  • 30% deposit required to secure booking. This is non-refundable unless your appointment is cancelled/rescheduled at least 24 hours prior to your appointment.
  • Appointments can only be rescheduled once without losing your deposit.
  • 15 minute grace period before the appointment is rescheduled.
  • No guests. Please keep pets and children in a separate room to avoid spillages/damage to property.
  • Please do not book if you have any of the following in/on/around the nails, fingers, hands (and arms/elbows if booking a manicure). You will lose your deposit (unless cancelled at least 24 hours before your appointment) and the treatment will not go ahead:
    • Cuts, abrasions, open sores, or broken skin
    • Numbness
    • Bruising or swelling
    • Separation of the nail plate from the nail bed
    • Scar tissue from recent injuries or surgeries
    • Extremely weak nails
    • Broken bones
    • Conditions like impetigo, scabies, chickenpox, or mumps (anywhere on the body)
  • Infills are only applicable within 2-3 weeks of original application depending on nail growth. If they are too grown out, chipping, or lifting, they must be soaked off and reapplied.
  • Infills will only be carried out twice before removal and reapplication to maintain natural nail health.
  • Infills & Soak Offs will only be carried out if the product was applied by Simmery Nails.
  • Please contact me if you have any health conditions prior to booking. Your desired treatment may not be suitable and an alternative can be offered.
  • Your health is of utmost importance.
